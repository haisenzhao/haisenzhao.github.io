Paper-related Open-source Projects

  • Chapper is a subtractive-manufacturing framework built around carvable hull-and-pack optimization, enabling geometry decomposition, packing, and fabrication planning under machining constraints.Project PageRepository
  • DeepMill uses neural accessibility learning to predict machining feasibility and guide tool-path-aware decision making for subtractive manufacturing workflows.Project PageRepository
  • Co-Optimization of Design and Fabrication Plans for Carpentry is a joint optimization framework that couples structural design, assembly layout, and fabrication planning for carpentry-aware digital fabrication.Project PageRepository

Other Open-source Repositories

  • Helm Compiler provides the implementation backbone for carpentry-oriented design compilation, translating fabrication-aware design decisions into executable construction logic.Repository
  • MIND is an open-source research codebase for geometry processing and modeling, supporting algorithm prototyping in our broader digital-geometry workflow.Repository
  • liblgp is a reusable geometry-processing library that packages low-level mesh operations, numerical utilities, and shared components used across multiple research systems.Repository
  • libhgp is a companion geometry-processing toolkit focused on reusable helpers and algorithmic building blocks for rapid development of fabrication and modeling pipelines.Repository
